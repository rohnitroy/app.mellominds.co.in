## FAQs

### Billing & Billing Cycle
**Q: When does billing start?**  
A: Billing starts on the day you upgrade to a paid plan. Your first invoice is pro-rated if you upgrade mid-month.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes. Individual & Team plans can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. Your access continues until the end of your billing cycle.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and netbanking via Razorpay or Cashfree.

### Plan Changes
**Q: Can I upgrade my plan?**  
A: Yes. Upgrade anytime; your first month of the new plan is pro-rated based on your current plan's cost.

**Q: Can I downgrade?**  
A: Yes. Downgrade anytime; the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens to my data when I downgrade?**  
A: Your data is never deleted. However, some features may be restricted (e.g., file attachments disabled on Free plan, client dashboard hidden on Individual plan).

### Team Plan Pricing
**Q: How does Team plan pricing work with multiple therapists?**  
A: Base price is ₹1,499/month per therapist seat. For 2 therapists, it's ₹2,998/month. You only pay for active seats.

**Q: Can I add/remove therapists mid-month?**  
A: Yes. Adding a therapist is pro-rated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Removing is effective immediately with no refund.

### Enterprise Plan
**Q: How much does Enterprise cost?**  
A: Enterprise pricing is custom based on your organization's size, features, and support needs. Contact sales for a quote.

**Q: What's included in Enterprise support?**  
A: Dedicated account manager, priority support (email, phone, Slack), quarterly business reviews, custom feature roadmap, and implementation assistance.
